Our Family Story

Founded in 2019 by Zirdrazieyal Davis and his wife Erica Davis, Top Notch Plumbing (TNP) began as a dream to provide exceptional plumbing services to our Paducah community. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the most trusted names in local plumbing, while maintaining the personal touch that only a family business can provide.
