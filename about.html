About Top Notch Plumbing (TNP)

Serving Paducah with world-class plumbing solutions since 2019

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by Zirdrazieyal Davis and his wife Erica Davis, Top Notch Plumbing (TNP) has grown from a small family business to become Paducah's most trusted plumbing service provider. What started as a local operation has evolved into a comprehensive plumbing solutions company that maintains the personal touch of a family business while delivering the expertise and reliability of a major corporation.

Our commitment to excellence, combined with deep local knowledge of Paducah's unique plumbing challenges, has made us the go-to choice for residential and commercial properties throughout Western Kentucky. We understand the specific needs of our community, from historic downtown buildings to modern suburban homes.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of satisfied customers across McCracken, Ballard, and Marshall counties, maintaining the same dedication to quality and customer service that we had when we first opened our doors. Our specialized expertise in new construction, sewer repair/replacement, and residential services sets us apart in the industry.

Our Mission

To provide world-class plumbing solutions with the personal touch of a local business, ensuring every customer receives exceptional service, fair pricing, and lasting solutions to their plumbing needs. We specialize in new construction for residential and commercial projects, sewer repair and replacement, and comprehensive residential services.
