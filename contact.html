Frequently Asked Questions

What are your emergency response times?

For emergency services, we typically respond within 1-2 hours. We prioritize emergencies and have technicians on call 24/7.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free estimates for most services. Emergency calls may have a service fee, but we'll always be transparent about costs.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. We're fully licensed by the Kentucky State Plumbing Board and carry comprehensive liability insurance for your protection.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards. We also offer financing options for larger projects.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties on both parts and labor for all our services.
